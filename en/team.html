About us

The Digital Transformation Team was born to build the "operating system" of the country, a series of fundamental components on top of which we can build simpler and more efficient services for the citizens, the Public Administration and businesses, through innovative digital products. The Commissioner's Team has been established on September 16, 2016 with an initial two-year mandate, up to September 16, 2018.
